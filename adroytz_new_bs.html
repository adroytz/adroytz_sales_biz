Our Legacy of Excellence

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

Established in 1993 as SS Computers and Consumables, we have been at the forefront of B2B technology solutions for over 30 years. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, cost-effective IT hardware and networking solutions to businesses across India.

Today, as Adroytz Technology Services LLP, we continue this legacy with renewed vision and expanded capabilities. Our rebranding reflects our commitment to innovation while maintaining the trust and reliability that our clients have come to expect.

With plans for nationwide expansion, we are positioned to serve businesses across India with the same dedication and expertise that has made us a trusted partner to industry leaders.
